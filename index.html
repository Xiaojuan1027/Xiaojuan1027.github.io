About Me

I am Xiaojuan Liu, a researcher focusing on urban ecology, land use change, economic risk assessment, and the relationship between people and nature. Here, I will introduce my research topics and their significance.

Research Areas

My research primarily focuses on the following areas:

  • Urban Ecology: Understanding the interactions between urban environments and natural ecosystems.
  • Land Use Change: Analyzing how land use patterns affect ecological and social outcomes.
  • Economic Risk Assessment: Evaluating the economic impacts of environmental changes.
  • People and Nature: Investigating the connections between human behavior and ecological health.
